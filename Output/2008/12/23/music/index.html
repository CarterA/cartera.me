Music!

Dec 23, 2008

Anyone noticed how many amazingly good albums have been released lately? Lets go through a few.

Guns N' Roses release their album Chinese Democracy. And pigs everywhere grew wings and flew. No, not kidding.

Papa Roach released their single, Hollywood Whore. Papa Roach is back. Everyone everywhere should shout for joy.

Buckcherry released a new album, Black Butterfly. It's really fucking good. Seriously.

Nickelback released Dark Horse. Really awesome.

Rise Against released Appeal To Reason. Buy it, ASAP.

Fall Out Boy released Folie Deux. If you haven't heard it, you need to.

Yep, those are my favorites of recent times. If you don't agree with them, don't say a word.
